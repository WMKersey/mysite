Bio

I have been crafting and creating since I was allowed to hold a pair of scissors. I would make anything out of just paper and tape. Over the past decade, I discovered the cosplay community, as well as endless resources of new materials, creating the opportunities to make anything I could imagine. Every cosplayer starts small. My first cosplay was black sweatpants and hoodie with foam posterboard, felt, and pom-poms hotglued together to look like Toothless from HTTYD. Now I'm designing my own patterns and styling my own wigs! To be honest, I never thought I'd get this far in my hobby as a cosplayer.
